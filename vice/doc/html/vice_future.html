Near future plans for the ports/GUIs of VICE

We currently have a lack of dedicated port/GUI maintainers (only SDL and BeOS/Haiku have dedicated maintainers), and because of that all other ports/GUIs are starting to lag behind. For this reason we are planning to switch all ports/GUIs (besides BeOS/Haiku) to SDL (1/2), and we will be working on trying to provide a native GTK3 GUI alternative for platforms that support it. If you are willing/able/interested in 'saving' your fav GUI, please send a mail to vice-emu-mail@lists.sourceforge.net. After the 3.2 release all ports/GUIs that do not have a dedicated maintainer will be phased out / converted to SDL (1/2).

For more information visit the main page.
